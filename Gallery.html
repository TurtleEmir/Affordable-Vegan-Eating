Get a closer look at the unique atmosphere, presentation, and plant-based creativity of Amsterdam West’s top vegan restaurants. All photos below correspond to the restaurants listed on the “Restaurants in Amsterdam West” page.

Vegan Sushi Bar

Creative plant-based sushi with colorful presentation.

  • Style: Modern, lively, minimalistic interior.
  • Visual focus: Artfully plated sushi rolls, sides, and desserts.

SOIL Vegan Café – West

Modern vegan café known for bold flavors and a cozy vibe.

  • Style: Neutral tones, plant décor, natural lighting.
  • Visual focus: Bowls, sandwiches, desserts, and casual dining.

Veganees

Asian-inspired vegan dishes full of color and variety.

  • Style: Urban, playful, and vibrant.
  • Visual focus: Bao buns, gyozas, stir-fries, group dining.

Health Corner

Healthy vegan café and juice bar.

  • Style: Calm, natural colors; small and cozy.
  • Visual focus: Juices, teas, and simple homemade meals.

Robin Food Kollektief

Community-based vegan kitchen focused on affordability and togetherness.

  • Style: Casual, communal, authentic.
  • Visual focus: Shared meals, volunteers, friendly dining tables.
