Vegan friendly grocery shops

Little Plant Pantry

A 100% plant-based, zero-waste grocery store and deli that focuses on wholefoods, organic products, and refill-friendly bulk shopping. You can buy grains, lentils, spices, vegan cheeses, and even freshly made vegan meals.

  • Address: Bosboom Toussaintstraat 45-H, 1054 AN Amsterdam (Oud-West)
  • Website: littleplantpantry.com (placeholder)
  • Price range: €1–€3 per 100 g (bulk foods), €5–€10 for ready meals
  • Highlights: Plastic-free shopping, local suppliers, refill options, friendly staff, and peaceful atmosphere.

Ekoplaza (Elandsgracht)

A well known organic supermarket offering a wide range of vegan-friendly items from plant-based milks and yogurts to fresh vegetables, snacks, and pantry essentials.

  • Address: Elandsgracht 118-1, 1016 VB Amsterdam
  • Website: ekoplaza.nl (placeholder)
  • Price range: Medium (€3–€10 depending on product)
  • Highlights: Convenient for everyday vegan groceries, strong organic selection, easily accessible from Amsterdam West.
