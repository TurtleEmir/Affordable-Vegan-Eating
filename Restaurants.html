Restaurants in Amsterdam West

Explore vegan-friendly spots in Amsterdam West that offer good quality and budget-friendly plant-based meals.

Vegan Sushi Bar

  • Creative plant-based sushi with colorful presentation and excellent reviews.
  • Address: Kinkerstraat 83H, Amsterdam (West).
  • Price range: Gyozas ≈ €8.95, Tempura ≈ €15.95 for lunch (approx.).
  • Highlights: Vibrant and inventive vegan sushi, stylish interior, highly rated service.

SOIL Vegan Café – West

  • Popular 100% vegan café known for its modern style and flavorful seasonal dishes.
  • Address: Bilderdijkstraat 141, Amsterdam – West.
  • Price range: Average meal ≈ €20.
  • Highlights: Comfort-food bowls, sandwiches, desserts, artisan beers; cozy, relaxed setting.

Veganees

  • Fully vegan, Asian-inspired restaurant with great service and taste.
  • Address: Wibautstraat 95, 1091 GK Amsterdam (check for West branch availability).
  • Menu: Stir-fries, bao buns, gyoza, and more.
  • Highlights: Vibrant menu ideal for sharing and trying a mix of Asian flavors.

Health Corner

  • Relaxed café offering healthy vegan meals, teas, and juices.
  • Address: Admiraal de Ruijterweg 54H, Amsterdam (De Baarsjes).
  • Price range: ≈ €14–16 for rice and vegetable dishes.
  • Highlights: Calm side-street location with Persian-inspired vegan meals and herbal drinks.

Robin Food Kollektief

  • Community-based vegan kitchen focused on affordability and homemade meals.
  • Address: Frederik Hendrikstraat 111A, 1052 HN Amsterdam (West).
  • Price range: 3-course meals ≈ €15.
  • Highlights: Volunteer-run, organic ingredients, communal dining focused on sustainability and inclusivity.
